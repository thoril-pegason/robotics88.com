What We Do

  • Decision-Making Algorithms for Robots

    We tailor a proprietary, generalized decision-making algorithm for robots to explore and collect data in diverse environments.

  • Data Stewardship

    We collect large amounts of ecological and robotic decision-making data for the purpose of better understanding the environment, climate change and the behaviors of robots.

  • Validation and Verification of Robotic Behavior

    With quantitative data, we ensure that robotic behavior is effectively understood and aligns with qualitative needs.

Current Projects

  • XPRISE Amazonas Action Alliance
